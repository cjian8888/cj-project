# Skill: 子智能体模拟 (Sub-agent Simulation)
## 触发指令 (Triggers)
- "分角色开发", "模拟团队", "P0-P5重构"

## Instruction
For complex refactoring:
1. Break down tasks into roles (e.g., "审计专员", "数据库专家").
2. Validate output from one role before passing to the next.
