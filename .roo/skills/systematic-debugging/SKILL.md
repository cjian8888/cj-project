# Skill: 系统化调试 (Systematic Debugging)
## 触发指令 (Triggers)
- "系统化排查", "调试模式", "深度Debug"

## Instruction
When facing complex bugs:
1. **提出假设 (Hypothesis)**: List 3 potential causes.
2. **验证计划 (Verification)**: Propose tests/logs for each.
3. **根因锁定 (Root Cause)**: Explain the "Why" before fixing.
