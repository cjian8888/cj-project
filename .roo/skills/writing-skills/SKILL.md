# Skill: 专业审计写作 (Professional Audit Writing)
## 触发指令 (Triggers)
- "写底稿", "润色报告", "生成审计文档"

## Tone & Style
- **Objective**: Neutral, evidence-based language (客观、中立).
- **Structure**: BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front - 结论先行).
- **Terminology**: Use precise financial audit terms (穿透、核销、关联方).
